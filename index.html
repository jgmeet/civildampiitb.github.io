DEPARTMENT ACADEMIC MENTORSHIP PROGRAMME BLOG

Civil Engineering Department, IIT Bombay

What is DAMP?

DAMP stands for Department Academic Mentorship Programme. As the name suggests, we help the students in their academic endeavours by providing them mentors from the department and at the same time work towards the academic enhancement of the department.

Here in this blog we would provide you the information on following topics:

Get Course Reviews

These are reviews of various courses of the department from the students who have already taken up the courses.

Know about the projects in institute

Information on the B.Tech and DDP projects taken up by various students in the department.

Know more about the future steps

This would have a list of various students who secured internships in various universities and companies, students who are going for higher studies and students who secured jobs in placements.

Tell us your Grievances

Any queries that you may have. We are happy to answer.
